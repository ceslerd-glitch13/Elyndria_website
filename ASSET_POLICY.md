# Public Token Asset Policy

Everything stored in this repository is public and may be downloaded by anyone.

## Allowed content

- Artwork you created.
- Artwork you purchased or licensed for this use.
- Public-domain or appropriately licensed artwork.
- Images that do not reveal private campaign text, statistics, or future plot details.

## Do not upload

- Character sheets, account information, passwords, or session data.
- Quest text, hidden notes, statistics, maps, or private archive material.
- Artwork that you do not have permission to display.
- SVG, HTML, scripts, executables, archives, or animated images.
- Filenames containing enemy names, encounter names, or campaign spoilers.

Original filenames are not published in the generated image index, but Git history can retain uploaded filenames. Use neutral source filenames when the filename itself would be a spoiler.

The campaign site must keep token assignments and visibility rules in its own protected database. This repository supplies public artwork only.
